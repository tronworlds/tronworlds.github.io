Tron Worlds

    Tutorial

    Install Tron Wallet

    For PC users we recomend to use Chrome browser and TronLink or similar extension. For mobile users Math Wallet or official TronLink Wallet are popular choices. But any mobile wallet that supports TRX and has build-in dapps browser should work.

    Buy some TRX

    If you don't own TRX then the most simple way to get it is to use CoinSwitch services. For Russia and neighbour countries BestChange is the right choice to find best prices for buying TRX

    Donate TRX to Tron World smart contract and buy some units

    Donate funds from your wallet to the smart contract and buy production units. They will give you resources every hour. One half of the resources goes to your in-game balance, you can buy another units with it and the other half goes to your withdrawable balance, you can take away it or buy another units as well.

    Type the amount, you want to put in into the field right before Top up button and press Top up

    It takes about one minute for Tron network to confirm transaction.

    Type the number of units, you want to buy into the field right before Buy button and press Buy

    All production units last till the next Rebirth. ATTENTION! Due to the nature of how smart contracts work all transaction are final and can't be refunded.

    TWGold

    You can also buy TWGold tokens with you your in-game and withdrawable balances. These tokens are used as the only currence for hiring Battle Heroes and buying equipment for them in the upcoming Game shop.

    Rebirth

    When one of the players take away more money that the contract balance has, the Rebirth starts. The game will be halted for 24 hours, all units and players balances will be destroyed and the game will start again. But all referral information, Battle Heroes and TWGold tokens will remain intact.
