Tron Worlds

    Tutorial

    Install Tron Wallet

    For PC users we recomend to use Chrome browser and TronLink or similar extension. For mobile users Math Wallet or official Tron Wallet are popular choices. But any mobile wallet that supports TRX and has build-in dapps browser should work.

    Buy some TRX

    If you don't own TRX then the most simple way to get it is to use CoinSwitch services. For Russia and neighbour countries BestChange is the right choice to find best prices for buying TRX

    Deposit TRX and buy some units

    Deposit funds from your wallet and buy production units. They will give you profit every hour. One half of the profit goes to your repurchase balance, you can buy another units with it and the other half goes to your withdrawable balance, you can withdraw it or reinvest as well.

    TWGold

    You can also buy TWGold tokens with you your repurchase balance. These tokens will be used as the only currence for upcoming Game shop, where you will be able to buy military units and equipment and join tournaments.

    Rebirth

    When one of the players withdraw more money that the contract balance has, the Rebirth starts. The game will be halted for 24 hours, all units and players balances will be destroyed and the game will start again. But all referral information and TWGold tokens will remain intact.
